Gmail Integration

BudgetCalculator can securely connect to your Gmail account to automatically import bank statement PDFs from Alta Bank. We only access specific bank statement emails and never read your personal correspondence.

What we access: Only emails from Alta Bank (izvod@altabanka.rs) containing monthly statements.

What we don't access: Any other emails, contacts, or personal information.

Privacy & Security

Your financial data is private and secure:

  • All transaction data is stored locally on your iOS device
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to external servers
  • Gmail access is read-only and limited to bank statements only
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings
